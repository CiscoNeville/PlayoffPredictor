In this folder there will be the following files:



ncfScoresFile.txt -  The scores of completed games in the current season
ncfScheduleFile.txt -  Games to be played this season

CurrentCalculatedRatings.txt - all teams with agaRatings and record to this point in the season, sorted by best agaRating
CurrentAveragedCommitteeBiasFile.txt - all teams, averaged bias for this season, no sort.
CurrentPredictedRankings.txt - top 25 teams,  calculated ratings + bias

CurrentPlayoffCommitteeRankings.txt - top 25 with a mapping of ratings to the rank for that week (should change)
